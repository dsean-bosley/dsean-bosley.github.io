The Practice for a Better Tommorrow

Positive Character Girls, Inc. partners with families to establish a better future for young girls through our mentoring services.
Vision Statement: Guided by the principles of proverbs 22:6, we strive to train and guide young girls, teens, and single parents to help ensure that they are successful in every area of their lives.

CORE VALUES

LOVE
Providing a caring, safe, and inviting environment that unconditionally cultivates positive self-esteem, creativity, imagination, and love of participant.
RESPECT
Appreciating and valuing each girl's individual background, culture, and abilities.
FAMILY
Promote an environment that supports and engages our diverse families to achieve their goals and honor them.
COMMUNITY
Engaging in mutually beneficial collaborations within the communities we serve to create positive sustainable outcomes.
SERVICE
Going beyond the basics, we extend ourselves by offering our efforts, resources, and talents to support those in need, with creative solutions with enthusiam.
